### Phase 2: Hook stdin parser + thinking record extractor
**Files:** `upfront/internal/hook/handler.go`, `upfront/internal/hook/handler_test.go`

Extractor uses regex for `### Thinking Record:` sections — captures phase name and summary text between heading and next `---` or `##`. Returns slice of Events.
